Corn Bread

Description

My grandmother often made thise for my family at holidays and also ith good homemade meals!

Ingredients

  • 1 package dry corn bread mix
  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons dried sage
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the dry corn bread mix according to package directions. Cool and crumble
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease one 9x13 inch baking dish
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and saute the celery and onion until soft
  4. In a larger bowl, combine the celery, onions, 3 cups crumbles corn bread, eggs, chicken stock, sage, and salt and pepper to taste; mix well.
  5. Place into prepared dish and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
